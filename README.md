# 🎮 Gamified Tracker

A **Gamified Productivity Tracker** that turns your daily tasks into a game.
Complete tasks, earn XP, level up, and track your productivity like a game character.

This project helps users stay motivated by combining **task management with gamification mechanics** such as XP, levels, streaks, and leaderboards.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🎯 **Task Tracking** – Manage and complete daily tasks
* ⚡ **XP System** – Earn experience points for completing tasks
* 🏆 **Level System** – Level up as your XP increases
* 🔥 **Daily Streaks** – Stay consistent and maintain streaks
* 📊 **Progress Tracking** – Visual progress with XP bars
* 🏅 **Leaderboard** – Compare progress with others
* 💾 **Local Storage** – Saves your progress automatically

## 🎮 How It Works

1. Add your daily tasks
2. Complete tasks to earn **XP**
3. XP increases your **level**
4. Maintain **daily streaks** for extra motivation
5. Compete on the **leaderboard**
